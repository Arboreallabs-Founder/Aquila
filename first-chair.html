Program Architecture

Three Pillars.
One Leader.

First Chair develops leadership holistically — mastering self, inspiring others, and driving business performance.

01

Pillar One

Lead Self

Build confidence, self-awareness, and executive presence to lead with authenticity and purpose.

  • Identity, intent & leadership style
  • Emotional intelligence & resilience
  • Executive presence & gravitas
  • Time, priority & energy management
  • Leadership voice & circle of influence
02

Pillar Two

Lead Others

Strengthen communication, coaching, collaboration, and influence to build high-performing teams.

  • Communicate with clarity & impact
  • Coaching & feedback skills
  • Conflict resolution & difficult conversations
  • Inclusive leadership & psychological safety
  • Motivation, delegation & team dynamics
03

Pillar Three

Lead Business

Develop business acumen, strategic thinking, decision-making, and execution capability.

  • Business acumen & financial literacy
  • Strategic thinking & goal setting
  • Decision-making & problem-solving
  • Accountability & follow-through
  • Capstone leadership project

The Curriculum

9 Modules. Real Skills.
Measurable Impact.

Each module is industry-led, application-focused, and tied to measurable behavioural outcomes.

Self

Identity & Leadership Intent

Clarify your leadership purpose, values, and authentic style. Own your narrative from day one.

Self

Emotional Intelligence & Resilience

Understand yourself, manage relationships, and lead through pressure with composure and consistency.

Self

Executive Presence & Influence

Build gravitas, credibility, and confidence across stakeholder levels — from team to boardroom.

Others

Communicate Like a Leader

Master clarity, structured messaging, and executive communication across formats and audiences.

Others

Coaching, Feedback & Motivation

Coach consistently, deliver feedback that drives growth, and inspire your team to perform at their best.

Others

Conflict & Difficult Conversations

Navigate conflict constructively, have courageous conversations, and build solutions under pressure.

Business

Business Acumen & Strategic Thinking

Understand financials, operations, and key business drivers. Think ahead, analyse scenarios, and create long-term impact.

Business

Goal Setting, Delegation & Accountability

Set clear goals aligned to business priorities, delegate effectively, and drive execution excellence.

Business

Capstone Leadership Project

Apply all learnings to a real business challenge. Present solutions to senior leaders. Demonstrate measurable impact.
