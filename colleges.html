Placement Formats

Two Placement Journeys,
Built Around Student Year

Separate pathways for first-year and second-year MBA students, with different intensity, timing, and readiness goals.

8-Week Final Sprint Bootcamp

For second-year MBA students approaching campus recruitment. Focused on resume optimisation, communication, aptitude, group discussions, domain interviews, behavioural interviews, mock interviews, and final simulations.

24-Week Placement Readiness System

For first-year MBA students. Uses checkpoints, scoring visibility, and a tiering system to identify placement-ready students, students who need polish, and students needing targeted intervention.

The Curriculum

8 Modules Covering
Every Recruitment Stage

One module per week progressive skill building across the full recruitment journey, delivered across 8 structured sessions.

01

Resume & LinkedIn Optimisation

ATS-ready resumes, keyword-rich LinkedIn profiles, and accomplishment-based bullet points tailored for HR, Finance, Marketing, and Consulting roles.

02

Communication & Executive Presence

Craft memorable self-introductions, structure answers with Present–Past–Future and SAO frameworks, and project credibility through body language and vocal delivery.

03

Quantitative & Verbal Aptitude

High-frequency arithmetic, logical reasoning, data interpretation, shortcut methods, and timed practice drills that simulate actual campus placement tests.

04

Group Discussions & Case Studies

POINT and Issue–Analysis–Recommendation frameworks, live simulated GDs, and facilitator feedback on content quality, leadership, and communication style.

05

Domain Interview Preparation

Role-specific preparation for HR, Finance, Marketing, Business Analytics, and Consulting connecting concepts to real business applications.

06

Behavioural Interview Preparation

STAR framework storytelling around leadership, teamwork, conflict, and achievement with a personal story bank built for your final placement season.

07

Mock Interviews

One-on-one mock interviews simulating HR, behavioural, and domain formats. Individualised feedback on content, structure, body language, and executive presence.

08

Final Placement Simulation

End-to-end panel interviews replicating real campus recruitment conditions final assessments, salary discussion strategies, and readiness scores.
