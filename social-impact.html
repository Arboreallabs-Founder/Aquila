Program 01 — Livelihood

Turning Local Skills Into
Lasting Income

Aquila's livelihood programs work within communities — particularly with women-led self-help groups — to build sustainable economic activity rooted in local skills, craft, fisheries, livestock, and entrepreneurship. The goal is income that outlasts the program, not just attendance certificates.

Women's SHG DevelopmentBuilding capacity within Self-Help Groups for collective enterprise, savings, and income-generating activity
Microenterprise IncubationSupporting local craft, food, textile, and service-based micro-businesses with business skills and market access
The Butterfly ProjectA flagship Section 8, 12A-80G certified initiative supporting women and youth across Jharkhand, Bihar, and UP
Land & Water LivelihoodsLivestock, dairy, poultry, goatery, piggery, fisheries, and aquaculture pathways led by sector experts
Market LinkagesConnecting local producers to markets, e-commerce platforms, and institutional buyers for sustained demand
Partner on Livelihood

Program 03 — Hire Train Deploy

From Unemployed to
Deployed in Logistics

India's logistics sector is one of the fastest-growing employers in the country — and chronically short of trained workers. Aquila's Hire-Train-Deploy model creates a reliable pipeline: we source underprepared candidates, run intensive domain training, and deploy them with guaranteed employment into partner logistics companies.

The 4-Step HTD Model

1
SourceIdentify underprepared but work-ready candidates from communities, ITIs, and referral networks
2
Screen & MatchAptitude and attitude screening to match candidates with the right logistics role profile
3
TrainRole-specific training: warehouse operations, last-mile delivery, documentation, safety, and soft skills
4
DeployGuaranteed placement with partner logistics companies, with 30-day post-joining support
