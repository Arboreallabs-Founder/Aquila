Workplace Safety & Compliance

POSH & Respectful Workplace Programs

Workplace safety is more than a policy requirement. It is a culture commitment.

Request a Conversation

Aquila's POSH programs help organisations create workplaces where employees understand their rights, responsibilities, and role in building a respectful culture. Our programs combine legal compliance, behavioural awareness, leadership accountability, and practical workplace application.

Designed for employees, managers, Internal Committees, and leadership teams, our workshops go beyond awareness sessions to drive meaningful behavioural change.

Our Approach

Four Outcomes That Define Effective POSH Training

We believe effective POSH training must achieve four outcomes:

Awareness

Help employees understand what constitutes sexual harassment, workplace boundaries, and acceptable conduct.

Confidence

Equip employees and managers to speak up, seek support, and respond appropriately when concerns arise.

Compliance

Ensure organisations meet statutory obligations under the POSH Act while strengthening internal processes.

Culture

Build workplaces rooted in dignity, inclusion, accountability, and mutual respect.

Programs We Offer
